Mercedes F1 Infringement Summary - 2021
==================================================

 Valtteri Bottas accused of driving unnecessarily slowly in the entry to turn 9 and 10 . Lewis Hamilton disqualified from qualifying after failing to meet the requirements of Art.6.3 of the 2021 FIA Formula 1 Technical Regulations . The Stewards held a hearing on Friday following qualifying with Ron Meadows, the Competitor representative, and Simon Cole, the Chief Engineer, Trackside and from the FIA Jo Bauer, Technical Delegate and Nicholas Tombazis, Single Seater Technical Director . The FIA has examined the design of the area of the car in question and are satisfied that the design meets the intent of the regulation .  The Stewards decide that car 44 failed the test indicated in TD/011- 19 and is therefore in breach of Art 3.6.3 of the FIA Formula 1 Technical Regulations . The FIA argues that while not regulatory, the TD, like many others, describes the procedure for the test so that competitors may design cars to meet the regulations . The Competitor alleged that the fact that the car passed the test in the center section of the wing is both a mitigating factor and shows that there was no intent to breach the regulation . However, the Stewards believe that which sections failed is not relevant to the fact the wing did fail the test .  The driver of car 44, Lewis Hamilton, undid his seat belts on the in-lap at the end of the race . The driver is fined €5,000 with an additional €20,000 which is suspended through 2022 . Valtteri Bottas collided with the rear of car 4 in the braking zone to turn 1, causing an avoidable collision in turn 1 . Bottas explained that he forgot to take off the WRAO t-shirt in time during the national anthem because of the onset of rain .  Lewis Hamilton and Valtteri Bottas have been involved in several incidents this season . Hamilton was reprimanded for failing to follow the Race Director’s instruction . Bottas was fined €25,000 by the Commercial Rights Holder following the decision . Hamilton will be forced to start the race from the rear of the grid from the back of the starting grid after Bottas failed to respect a single yellow flag in the first session of the Formula One season. Bottas will be banned from qualifying for the first time since 2009 .
