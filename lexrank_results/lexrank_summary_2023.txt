Mercedes F1 Infringement Summary - 2023
==================================================

2023 ABU DHABI GRAND PRIX 24 - 26 November 2023 Having been delegated, by Stewards of 2023 Las Vegas Grand Prix, authority in accordance with Article 11.9.3.w of FIA International Sporting Code, and having received delegated authority from USA ASN (ACCUS) to UAE ASN steward, to investigate report from FIA Media Delegate in relation to language used by Team Principal of Mercedes- AMG Petronas F1 Team during FIA Press Conference on Thursday 16 November 2023, and having summonsed Team Principal (document 6) and having received submission from FIA and examined transcript of relevant part of Press Conference, Stewards determine that; 1. breach of Article 12.2.1.f of FIA International Sporting Code has been committed by Team Principal in that language used is not consistent with values defended by FIA; 2. Decision No further action Reason Stewards having heard from team representative and receiving medical report from team doctor of Mercedes-AMG PETRONAS F1 Team, which requested exemption from compliance with Art. No / Driver 44 - Lewis Hamilton Session Practice 1 Fact Pit lane speeding – 80.2 km/h Infringement Breach of Article 34.7 of FIA Formula One Sporting Regulations. team representative noted that his driver’s schedule was controlled by team, and given that this was his home race, schedule was extremely busy. Decision No further action.
