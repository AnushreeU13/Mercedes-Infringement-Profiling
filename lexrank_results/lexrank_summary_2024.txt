Mercedes F1 Infringement Summary - 2024
==================================================

Infringement Breach of Appendix L, Chapter IV Article 4 d) of the International Sporting Code and non-compliance with Race Director’s Event Notes (item 12.2, document 16). Therefore Car 44 is required to start the Race from pit lane in accordance with Article 40.9. Decision Reprimand (Driving). Reason The Stewards heard from the driver of Car 63 (George Russell), team representative and reviewed positioning/marshalling system data, video, telemetry and in-car video evidence. Russell was the next car on track and was fully committed to his braking, and unlike Leclerc, was already turning into the corner, when the yellow flag was displayed on the far right of the runoff.
