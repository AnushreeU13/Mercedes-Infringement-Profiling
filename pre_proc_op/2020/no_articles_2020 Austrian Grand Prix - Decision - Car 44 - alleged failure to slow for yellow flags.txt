From Stewards To Team Manager, Mercedes-AMG Petronas F1 TeamDocument 33 Date 04 July 2020 Time 19:44 2020 AUSTRIAN GRAND PRIX 2 - 5 July 2020 StewardsThe Stewards, having received report from Race Director, summoned (document 29) and heard from driver and team representative, have considered following matter and determine following: No / Driver 44 - Lewis Hamilton Competitor Mercedes-AMG Petronas F1 Team Time 15:59 Session Qualifying Fact Alleged failure to slow for single waved yellow flags between turn 5 and 7. Offence Alleged breach of Appendix H Article 2.5.5.1.b) of FIA International Sporting Code. Decision No further action. Reason Stewards heard from driver of Car 44 (Lewis Hamilton) and team representative and have reviewed video and telemetry evidences. driver mentioned that he passed green light panel in turn 5. video footage confirmed that there have been yellow flags and green light panels at same time and therefore conflicting signals were shown to driver. Taking this into account, Stewards decide to take no further action. Competitors are reminded that they have right to appeal certain decisions of Stewards, in accordance with Article 15 of FIA International Sporting Code and Article 10.1.1 of FIA Judicial and Disciplinary Rules, within applicable time limits. Gerd Ennser Felix Holter Vitantonio Liuzzi Walter Jobst
