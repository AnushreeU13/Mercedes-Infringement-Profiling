From Stewards To Team Manager, Mercedes-AMG Petronas F1 TeamDocument 41 Date 05 July 2020 Time 14:24 2020 AUSTRIAN GRAND PRIX 2 - 5 July 2020 Stewards received petition from Aston Martin Red Bull Racing (including video footage) for them to review, in accordance with Article 14 of FIA International Sporting Code, following decision made by Stewards at 2020 Austrian Grand Prix Competition: Document 33 – 2020 Austrian Grand Prix. Stewards, summoned team (Document 39) and held hearing at 13:45 on Sunday 5th July, 2020. Stewards heard driver and team representative. Stewards determine that additional video evidence represents significant and relevant new element which was unavailable to parties at time of competition concerned. Reasons: During initial hearing, no on-board video footage of car 44 was available to Stewards. Aston Martin Red Bull submitted 360 degree camera footage from car 44 including passage of car 44 through relevant section of track. StewardsGerd Ennser Felix Holter Vitantonio Liuzzi Walter Jobst
