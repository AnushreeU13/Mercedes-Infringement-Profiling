From Stewards To Team Manager, Mercedes-AMG Petronas F1 TeamDocument 32 Date 04 July 2020 Time 19:43 2020 AUSTRIAN GRAND PRIX 2 - 5 July 2020 StewardsThe Stewards, having received report from Race Director, summoned (document 28) and heard from driver and team representative, have considered following matter and determine following: No / Driver 44 - Lewis Hamilton Competitor Mercedes-AMG Petronas F1 Team Time 15:52 Session Qualifying Fact Car 44 did not use track at exit of turn 10. Offence Breach of Appendix L, Ch IV, Article 2c of FIA International Sporting Code and failure to follow Race Director’s Event Notes 21.2.a). Decision Deletion of lap time (1:03.061) in accordance with Article 12.3.1.e of FIA International Sporting Code and Art 31.4 of FIA Formula One Sporting Regulations. Reason Stewards heard from driver of Car 44 (Lewis Hamilton) and team representative and have reviewed video, evidence. car left track. As no lap time has been achieved in lap following infringement, only lap time in which infringement had happened is being deleted. penalty was applied in accordance with point 21.2.a) of Race Director’s Event Notes and Article 12.3.1.e of FIA International Sporting Code. Competitors are reminded that they have right to appeal certain decisions of Stewards, in accordance with Article 15 of FIA International Sporting Code and Article 10.1.1 of FIA Judicial and Disciplinary Rules, within applicable time limits.
