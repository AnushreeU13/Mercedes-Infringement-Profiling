From Stewards To Team Manager, Mercedes-AMG Petronas F1 TeamDocument 50 Date 05 July 2020 Time 17:16 2020 AUSTRIAN GRAND PRIX 2 - 5 July 2020 StewardsThe Stewards, having received report from Race Director, have considered following matter and determine following: No / Driver 44 - Lewis Hamilton Competitor Mercedes-AMG Petronas F1 Team Time 16:31 Session Race Fact Car 44 and car 23 collided in turn 4. Offence Involved in incident defined by Article 38.1 of FIA Formula One Sporting Regulations. Decision 5 second time penalty. 2 penalty points imposed, total of 7 in 12 month period Reason Stewards reviewed video evidence showing that cars 23 and 44 were side by side approaching apex of turn 4. They negotiated turn side by side, but car 23 had better exit and was in process of passing car 44. Car 44 was drafting to outside at exit of turn 4 and consequently making contact with rear right wheel of car 23, causing car 23 to spin. Stewards determined that driver of car 44 is predominantly to blame for collision. Competitors are reminded that they have right to appeal certain decisions of Stewards, in accordance with Article 15 of FIA International Sporting Code and Article 10.1.1 of FIA Judicial and Disciplinary Rules, within applicable time limits.
