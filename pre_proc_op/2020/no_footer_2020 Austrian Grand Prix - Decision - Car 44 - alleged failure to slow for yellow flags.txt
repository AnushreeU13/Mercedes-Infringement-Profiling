No / Driver 44 - Lewis Hamilton Session Qualifying Fact Alleged failure to slow for single waved yellow flags between turn 5 and 7. Offence Alleged breach of Appendix H Article 2.5.5.1.b) of FIA International Sporting Code. Decision No further action. Reason Stewards heard from driver of Car 44 (Lewis Hamilton) and team representative and have reviewed video and telemetry evidences. driver mentioned that he passed green light panel in turn 5. video footage confirmed that there have been yellow flags and green light panels at same time and therefore conflicting signals were shown to driver. Taking this into account, Stewards decide to take no further action.
