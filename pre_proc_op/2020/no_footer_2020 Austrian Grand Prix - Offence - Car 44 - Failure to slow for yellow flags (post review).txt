No / Driver 44 - Lewis Hamilton Session Qualifying Fact Failure to slow for single waved yellow flags between turn 5 and 7. Offence Breach of Appendix H Article 2.5.5.1.b) of FIA International Sporting Code. Decision Drop of 3 grid positions. (2 penalty points awarded, 5 points in total for 12 month period) Reason Following petition to review decision 33 taken after Qualifying, Stewards acknowledged that on-board footage of car 44 represents significant new element (see decision 41) that had not been available to Stewards in hearing on Saturday and therefore reviewed case. Stewards heard from driver of Car 44 (Lewis Hamilton) and team representative and have reviewed new video evidence and telemetry evidences. new video footage clearly shows that yellow light panel was flashing on left side of track in turn 5. green light panel was flashing at end of marshalling sector 9. Taking into account these facts, Stewards determine that decision 33 will be reversed and above mentioned penalty is being imposed. Gerd Ennser Felix Holter Vitantonio Liuzzi Walter Jobst
