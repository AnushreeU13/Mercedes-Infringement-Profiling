No / Driver 44 - Lewis Hamilton Session Race Fact Car 44 and car 23 collided in turn 4. Offence Involved in incident defined by Article 38.1 of FIA Formula One Sporting Regulations. Decision 5 second time penalty. 2 penalty points imposed, total of 7 in 12 month period Reason Stewards reviewed video evidence showing that cars 23 and 44 were side by side approaching apex of turn 4. They negotiated turn side by side, but car 23 had better exit and was in process of passing car 44. Car 44 was drafting to outside at exit of turn 4 and consequently making contact with rear right wheel of car 23, causing car 23 to spin. Stewards determined that driver of car 44 is predominantly to blame for collision.
