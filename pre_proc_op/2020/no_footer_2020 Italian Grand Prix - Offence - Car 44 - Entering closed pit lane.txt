No / Driver 44 - Lewis Hamilton Session Race Fact Entered pit lane when it was closed. Offence Breach of Article 28.14 of FIA Formula One Sporting Regulations. Decision 10 second stop and go penalty (2 penalty points imposed, total of 8 points in 12-month period). Reason Stewards viewed video evidence, noted that pit lane was closed at 15:41:47. pit lane closed message was displayed on Time Page 3, and pit lane closed lights were displayed on light panels 16 and 17 at same time. Car 44 entered pit lane 15:41:59 We note that this is mandatory penalty under Sporting Regulations.
