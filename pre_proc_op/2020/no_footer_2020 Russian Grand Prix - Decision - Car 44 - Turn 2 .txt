No / Driver 44 - Lewis Hamilton Session Qualifying Fact Alleged breach of FIA International Sporting Code Article 12.1.1 (i), failure to follow instructions contained in Event Notes (v3) item 22.1 in turn 2 at 15:04:43. Offence Alleged breach of Article 12.1.1.(i) of FIA International Sporting Code. Decision No further action. Reason Stewards heard from driver of Car 44 (Lewis Hamilton) and team representative and have reviewed video evidence. driver left track at turn 2 and did not manage to re-join track as defined in Event Notes (v3) 22.1. driver accepted that he had not followed instructions and further he accepted that in race there would be penalty. Stewards determined that there was no advantage, as relevant lap time was deleted according to Doc 27. Furthermore, to be consistent with previous decisions, Stewards considered precedents set out in Doc. 25 of 2018 Spanish Gran Prix and Doc. 31 of 2019 Canadian Grand Prix. Stewards remind all teams and drivers, that failure to negotiate turn 2 as defined in Event Notes during race will likely lead to 5 sec time penalty.
