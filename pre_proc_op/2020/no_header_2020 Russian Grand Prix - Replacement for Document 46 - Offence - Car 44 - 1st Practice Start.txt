No / Driver 44 - Lewis Hamilton Competitor Mercedes-AMG Petronas F1 Team Time 13:32 Session Reconnaissance Laps Fact Practice start violation. Offence Breach of FIA International Sporting Code Article 12.1.1 (i), failure to follow instructions contained in Event Notes (v3) item 19.1, practice start location at 13:32 and Article 36.1 FIA Formula One Sporting Regulations. Decision 5 second time penalty competitor (Mercedes-AMG Petronas F1 Team) is fined € 25,000. Reason Stewards received information from team that driver of car 44 had received team instruction to perform practice start in incorrect place. This was confirmed by Stewards having listened to audio between Team and Driver. Based on this information Stewards replace document 46 with this decision and therefore remove penalty points imposed and fine competitor (Mercedes-AMG Petronas F1 Team) € 25,000. Competitors are reminded that they have right to appeal certain decisions of Stewards, in accordance with Article 15 of FIA International Sporting Code and Article 10.1.1 of FIA Judicial and Disciplinary Rules, within applicable time limits.
