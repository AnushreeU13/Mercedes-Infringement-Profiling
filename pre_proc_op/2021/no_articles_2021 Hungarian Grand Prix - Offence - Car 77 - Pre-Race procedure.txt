From Stewards To Team Manager, Mercedes-AMG Petronas F1 TeamDocument 51 Date 01 August 2021 Time 18:56 2021 HUNGARIAN GRAND PRIX 29 July - 1 August 2021 StewardsThe Stewards, having received report from Race Director, summoned (document 45) and heard from driver and team representative, have considered following matter and determine following: No / Driver 77 - Valtteri Bottas Competitor Mercedes-AMG Petronas F1 Team Time 14:44 Session Race Fact Did not follow Race Director’s Instruction. Offence Breach of Article 12.2.1.i) of FIA International Sporting Code. Decision Reprimand (Non-driving) (This is driver’s 1st reprimand of season) Reason Stewards heard from driver of Car 77 (Valtteri Bottas) and team representative. driver explained that he forgot to take off WRAO t-shirt in time during national anthem because of onset of rain. Competitors are reminded that they have right to appeal certain decisions of Stewards, in accordance with Article 15 of FIA International Sporting Code and Article 10.1.1 of FIA Judicial and Disciplinary Rules, within applicable time limits.
