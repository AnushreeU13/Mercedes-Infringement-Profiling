From Stewards To Team Manager, Mercedes-AMG Petronas F1 TeamDocument 17 Date 05 November 2021 Time 13:20 2021 M EXICAN GRAND PRIX 4 - 7 November 2021 StewardsThe Stewards, having received report from Race Director, summoned (document 9) and heard from driver and team representative, have considered following matter and determine following: No / Driver 44 - Lewis Hamilton Competitor Mercedes-AMG Petronas F1 Team Time 11:46 Session Practice 1 Fact Failed to follow Race Director’s Instruction. Offence Breach of Article 12.2.2.1.i) of FIA International Sporting Code. Decision Reprimand (Driving) (This is driver’s 1st reprimand of season) Reason Stewards heard from driver of Car 44 (Lewis Hamilton) and team representative. Car 44 left track on left hand-side between turns 1 and 2 and re-joined track between turns 2 and 3, rather than re-joining by driving to left-hand side of bollard at turn 3. Whilst noting driver’s comments in relation to track conditions and his attempt to steer to left after leaving track, Stewards consider this to be failure to follow Race Director’s instruction given in article 21.1a) of Race Director’s Event Notes. Competitors are reminded that they have right to appeal certain decisions of Stewards, in accordance with Article 15 of FIA International Sporting Code and Chapter 4 of FIA Judicial and Disciplinary Rules, within applicable time limits.
