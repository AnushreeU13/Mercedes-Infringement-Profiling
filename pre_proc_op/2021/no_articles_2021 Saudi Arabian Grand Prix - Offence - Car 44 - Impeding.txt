From Stewards To Team Manager, Mercedes-AMG Petronas F1 TeamDocument 21 Date 04 December 2021 Time 20:02 2021 SAUDI ARABIAN GRAND PRIX 2 - 5 December 2021 Stewards, having received report from Race Director, summoned (document 19) and heard from driver and team representative, have considered following matter and determine following: No / Driver 44 - Lewis Hamilton Competitor Mercedes-AMG Petronas F1 Team Time 17:39 Session Practice 3 Fact Unnecessarily impeding of Car 9 by Car 44 in Turn 8 Offence Breach of Article 31.5 of FIA Formula One Sporting Regulations. Decision Reprimand (Driving) (This is 2nd Reprimand of season) fine of €25,000 is imposed on Team Reason Stewards heard from driver of Car 44 (Lewis Hamilton), team representative and examined video and team radio evidence. driver was given 10 second warning that Car 9 was approaching when he was at Turn 2. Due to failure in communication by Team he was not given another warning until Car 9 was alongside him. Stewards accept that this circuit presents challenges for drivers in relation to using their mirrors as method of determining approach of overtaking cars. Although it is driver’s ultimate responsibility to avoid impeding, for this circuit driver must depend upon Team to communicate efficiently. This did not happen in this case and accordingly penalty for Competitor is imposed. Stewards take this opportunity to emphasise that due to nature of this circuit it is essential that teams communicate effectively and proactively with their drivers. This is not to be taken as precedent for other circuits. Competitors are reminded that they have right to appeal certain decisions of Stewards, in accordance with Article 15 of FIA International Sporting Code and Chapter 4 of FIA Judicial and Disciplinary Rules, within applicable time limits.
