No / Driver 77 - Valtteri Bottas Session Qualifying Fact Alleged driving unnecessarily slowly in entry to turn 9 and 10. Offence Alleged breach of Article 27.4 of FIA Formula One Sporting Regulations. Decision No further action. Reason Stewards heard from driver of Car 77 (Valtteri Bottas) and team representative and reviewed video evidence. At end of Q2 BOT slowed down at entry of T9 to create gap for his final lap. This is contrary to Race Directors event notes 24.2a. However, given situation that many drivers ended up queuing at this part of circuit, Stewards determine that too many drivers contributed to situation and therefore driver concerned is not fully to blame. Competitors are reminded that they have right to appeal certain decisions of Stewards, in accordance with Article 15 of FIA International Sporting Code and Article 10.1.1 of FIA Judicial and Disciplinary Rules, within applicable time limits.
