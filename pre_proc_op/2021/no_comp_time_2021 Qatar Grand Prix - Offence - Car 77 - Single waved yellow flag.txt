No / Driver 77 - Valtteri Bottas Session Qualifying Fact Failure to respect single yellow flag. Offence Breach of Appendix H, Art. 2.5.5.b) of FIA International Sporting Code and non- compliance with Race Director’s Event Notes 7.2 . Decision Drop of 3 grid positions (1 penalty point imposed, total of 5 points in 12 month period) Reason Stewards heard from driver of Car 77 (Valtteri Bottas), team representative and have reviewed video, telemetry and marshalling system evidence. driver admitted he did not notice yellow flag displayed at Flag Point 16.6 and conceded that he did not reduce speed as required in yellow flag area despite noting that Car 10 was stationary on pit straight. Competitors are reminded that they have right to appeal certain decisions of Stewards, in accordance with Article 15 of FIA International Sporting Code and Article 10.1.1 of FIA Judicial and Disciplinary Rules, within applicable time limits.
