No / Driver 44 - Lewis Hamilton Session Race Fact Undid safety belts while in motion on track. Offence Breach of Appendix L Chapter III Article 4 of FIA International Sporting Code. Decision driver is fined €5,000 with additional €20,000 which is suspended through end of 2022. Reason Stewards heard from team representative and reviewed video evidence. driver of car 44, Lewis Hamilton, undid his seat belts on in-lap at end of race. While Stewards are sympathetic to desire to celebrate, it is fundamentally unsafe to undo seatbelts while car is in motion. Slow speeds in these cars are very fast for unrestrained occupant. Further, Formula 1 drivers set example for junior categories. It is critical that junior category drivers learn importance of using all safety devices of car at all times. suspension of penalty is subject to compliance with Appendix L, Ch. III of ISC during period specified.
