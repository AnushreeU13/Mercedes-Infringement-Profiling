No / Driver 44 - Lewis Hamilton Session Practice 1 Fact Failed to follow Race Director’s Instruction. Offence Breach of Article 12.2.2.1.i) of FIA International Sporting Code. Decision Reprimand (Driving) (This is driver’s 1st reprimand of season) Reason Stewards heard from driver of Car 44 (Lewis Hamilton) and team representative. Car 44 left track on left hand-side between turns 1 and 2 and re-joined track between turns 2 and 3, rather than re-joining by driving to left-hand side of bollard at turn 3. Whilst noting driver’s comments in relation to track conditions and his attempt to steer to left after leaving track, Stewards consider this to be failure to follow Race Director’s instruction given in article 21.1a) of Race Director’s Event Notes.
