No / Driver 44 - Lewis Hamilton Session Practice 3 Fact Alleged failure to respect double yellow flags/lights. Offence Alleged breach of Appendix H, Art. 2.5.5.b) of FIA International Sporting Code. Decision No further action. Reason Stewards heard from driver of Car 44 (Lewis Hamilton), team representative and have reviewed video and marshalling system. Race Director reported to Stewards that double yellow flag warning on FIA Marshalling System was activated at Light Panel Number 6 accidentally, for less than 1 second. As on-board video of Car 44 (to be released by Commercial Rights Holder following this Decision) clearly shows, there was no yellow flag displayed, no yellow lights were displayed to that driver and yellow warning light was not visible on driver’s steering wheel. Unlike other incidents this year, there was no yellow flag or yellow light displayed to driver driver already being well into marshalling sector when system was briefly activated) hence no breach of regulations has occurred.
