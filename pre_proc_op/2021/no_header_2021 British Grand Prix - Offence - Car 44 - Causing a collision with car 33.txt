No / Driver 44 - Lewis Hamilton Competitor Mercedes-AMG Petronas F1 Team Time 15:04 Session Race Fact Causing collision with car 33 in turn 9. Offence Breach of Article 2 d) Chapter IV Appendix L of FIA International Sporting Code. Decision 10 second time penalty 2 Penalty Points (4 points in 12 month period) Reason Stewards reviewed video and telemetry evidence. Cars 33 and 44 entered turn 9 with Car 33 in lead and Car 44 slightly behind and on inside. Car 44 was on line that did not reach apex of corner, with room available to inside. When Car 33 turned into corner, Car 44 did not avoid contact and left front of Car 44 contacted right rear of Car 33. Car 44 is judged predominantly at fault. Competitors are reminded that they have right to appeal certain decisions of Stewards, in accordance with Article 15 of FIA International Sporting Code and Article 10.1.1 of FIA Judicial and Disciplinary Rules, within applicable time limits.
