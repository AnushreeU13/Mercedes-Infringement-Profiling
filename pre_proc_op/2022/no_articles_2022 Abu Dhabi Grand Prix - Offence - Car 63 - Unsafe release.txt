From Stewards To Team Manager, Mercedes-AMG Petronas F1 TeamDocument 32 Date 20 November 2022 Time 17:45 2022 ABU DHABI GRAND PRIX 18 - 20 November 2022 StewardsThe Stewards, having received report from Race Director, have considered following matter and determine following: No / Driver 63 - George Russell Competitor Mercedes-AMG Petronas F1 Team Time 17:26 Session Race Fact Unsafe release. Offence Breach of Article 34.14 of FIA Formula One Sporting Regulations. Decision 5 second time penalty. Reason Stewards reviewed multiple angles of video evidence. Car 63 was released from pit stop position into path of NOR who had to take evasive action to avoid collision. Competitors are reminded that they have right to appeal certain decisions of Stewards, in accordance with Article 15 of FIA International Sporting Code and Chapter 4 of FIA Judicial and Disciplinary Rules, within applicable time limits.
