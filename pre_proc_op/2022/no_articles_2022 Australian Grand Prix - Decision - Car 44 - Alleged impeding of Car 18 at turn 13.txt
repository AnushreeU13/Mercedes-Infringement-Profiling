From Stewards To Team Manager, Mercedes-AMG Petronas F1 TeamDocument 30 Date 08 April 2022 Time 20:41 2022 AUSTRALIAN GRAND PRIX 7 - 10 April 2022 StewardsThe Stewards, having received report from Race Director, summoned (documents 21 & 23) and heard from drivers and team representatives, have considered following matter and determine following: No / Driver 44 - Lewis Hamilton Competitor Mercedes-AMG Petronas F1 Team Time 16:05 Session Practice 2 Fact Impeding of Car 18 by Car 44 at turn 13. Offence Alleged breach of Article 37.5 of FIA Formula One Sporting Regulations. Decision No further action. Reason Stewards heard from driver of Car 44 (Lewis Hamilton), driver of Car 18 (Lance Stroll) and team representatives and examined video evidence. Due to presence of multiple cars converging on final corners, all of which impacted situation, Stewards consider that Car 44 did not unnecessarily impede Car 18 and take no further action. Competitors are reminded that they have right to appeal certain decisions of Stewards, in accordance with Article 15 of FIA International Sporting Code and Chapter 4 of FIA Judicial and Disciplinary Rules, within applicable time limits.
