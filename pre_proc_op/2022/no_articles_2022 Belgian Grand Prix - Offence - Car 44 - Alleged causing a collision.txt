From Stewards To Team Manager, Mercedes-AMG Petronas F1 TeamDocument 65 Date 28 August 2022 Time 15:53 2022 BELGIAN GRAND PRIX 25 - 28 August 2022 StewardsThe Stewards, having received report from Race Director, have considered following matter and determine following: No / Driver 44 - Lewis Hamilton Competitor Mercedes-AMG Petronas F1 Team Time 15:04 Session Race Fact Collision with car 14 at turn 5. Offence Breach of Article 2 d) Chapter IV Appendix L of FIA International Sporting Code. Decision No further action. Reason Stewards reviewed video evidence and determined that Alonso was on inside at turn 5. Hamilton’s front wheels were ahead of Alonso’s at entry to corner. Alonso moved his car off line to inside with both right side tyres fully on kerb and even somewhat inside kerb. At no point did Alonso appear to lose control or understeer. Hamilton turned into towards apex of corner with Alonso still along side and collision occurred. Stewards considered that this was first lap incident with lot of movement relative to other cars in first few corners, and thus take no further action. Competitors are reminded that they have right to appeal certain decisions of Stewards, in accordance with Article 15 of FIA International Sporting Code and Chapter 4 of FIA Judicial and Disciplinary Rules, within applicable time limits.
