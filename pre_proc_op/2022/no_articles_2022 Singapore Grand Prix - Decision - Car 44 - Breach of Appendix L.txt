From Stewards To Team Manager, Mercedes-AMG Petronas F1 TeamDocument 38 Date 01 October 2022 Time 23:19 2022 SINGAPORE GRAND PRIX 29 September - 02 October 2022 StewardsThe Stewards, having received report from Race Director, summoned (Document 30) and heard from driver and team representative, have considered following matter and determine following: No / Driver 44 - Lewis Hamilton Competitor Mercedes-AMG Petronas F1 Team Time 18:30 Session Practice 3 Fact Wearing of jewellery during competition. Offence Breach of Appendix L, Chapter III of FIA Formula One Sporting Regulations. Decision No further action. Reason Stewards heard from driver of Car 44 (Lewis Hamilton), team representative and have reviewed video and audio evidence. Broadcast footage showed HAM wearing item of jewellery in form of body piercing (nose stud) during session. HAM admitted this fact but explained that he had been advised by his doctors not to remove it for time being. In response to request by Stewards, team produced reports from medical practitioner which confirmed HAM’s explanation. Stewards then consulted FIA Deputy Medical Delegate, Dr Ian Roberts, who viewed medical report and concurred with opinion therein. In light of extenuating circumstances, we have determined to take no further action. Competitors are reminded that they have right to appeal certain decisions of Stewards, in accordance with Article 15 of FIA International Sporting Code and Chapter 4 of FIA Judicial and Disciplinary Rules, within applicable time limits.
