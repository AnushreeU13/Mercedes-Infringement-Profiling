From Stewards To Team Manager, Mercedes-AMG Petronas F1 TeamDocument 42 Date 23 October 2022 Time 14:23 2022 UNITED STATES GRAND PRIX 20 - 23 October 2022 StewardsThe Stewards, having received report from Race Director, have considered following matter and determine following: No / Driver 63 - George Russell Competitor Mercedes-AMG Petronas F1 Team Time 14:03 Session Race Fact Incident with car 55 and Car 63 in turn 1. Offence Breach of Article 2 d) Chapter IV Appendix L of FIA International Sporting Code. Decision 5 second time penalty 2 penalty points (total of 4 for 12 month period). Reason Stewards reviewed video evidence and determined that driver of car 63 braked late into turn 1 and collided with car 55, and was therefore wholly to blame for collision. Competitors are reminded that they have right to appeal certain decisions of Stewards, in accordance with Article 15 of FIA International Sporting Code and Chapter 4 of FIA Judicial and Disciplinary Rules, within applicable time limits.
