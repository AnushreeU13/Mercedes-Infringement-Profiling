No / Driver 44 - Lewis Hamilton Session Race Fact Driver Assistants in Parc Ferme. Offence Breach of Article 12.2.1 i) of International Sporting Code. Decision Fine of €10,000 – Suspended. Reason Stewards received report from Media Delegate, which was subsequently confirmed by video evidence, that physio / drivers’ assistants of top three finishers entered parc ferme without permission and in violation of procedure that was published prior to race “for orderly conduct of event.” In part this is to prevent handing over of items to drivers prior to them being weighed. Competitor is fined €10,000, which will be suspended for remainder of season pending any further violation of procedure, and competitors are warned that passes of individuals concerned may be revoked in case of systemic violation. Competitors are reminded that they have right to appeal certain decisions of Stewards, in accordance with Article 15 of FIA International Sporting Code and Chapter 4 of FIA Judicial and Disciplinary Rules, within applicable time limits.
