No / Driver 44 - Lewis Hamilton Session Practice 2 Fact Impeding of Car 18 by Car 44 at turn 13. Offence Alleged breach of Article 37.5 of FIA Formula One Sporting Regulations. Decision No further action. Reason Stewards heard from driver of Car 44 (Lewis Hamilton), driver of Car 18 (Lance Stroll) and team representatives and examined video evidence. Due to presence of multiple cars converging on final corners, all of which impacted situation, Stewards consider that Car 44 did not unnecessarily impede Car 18 and take no further action.
