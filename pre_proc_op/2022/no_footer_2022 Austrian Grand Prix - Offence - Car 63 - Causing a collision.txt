No / Driver 63 - George Russell Session Race Fact Collision with car 11 at turn 4. Offence Breach of Article 2 d) Chapter IV Appendix L of FIA International Sporting Code. Decision 5 second time penalty. 2 penalty points (total of 3 for 12 month period). Reason Stewards reviewed video evidence and determined that car 63 was wholly at fault for incident. Car 11 was passing on outside at turn 4, was at least fully alongside at entry of turn and all way through manoeuvre, including at apex of corner and left sufficient room for car 63, which ultimately struck car 11. This was not considered as “first lap incident” as this was battle between two cars and was not affected by pack of cars as can happen during first lap.
