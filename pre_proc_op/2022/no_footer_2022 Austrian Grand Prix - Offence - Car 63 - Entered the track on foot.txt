No / Driver 63 - George Russell Session Qualifying Fact driver of car 63 entered track on foot at 18:09. Offence Breach of Article 12.2.1.h) of FIA International Sporting Code and/or Article 26.7 b) of FIA Formula One Sporting Regulations. Decision Warning for driver. Reason Stewards heard from driver of Car 63 (George Russell) and team representatives and examined video evidence. Russell had just crashed in turn 10 and caused red flag. He then crossed track on foot and entered section of pit lane that does not have speed limit, without permission from any marshals. At time he started crossing track, some of cars were still on track and could possibly have missed pit entry, causing unsafe situation. stewards spoke to driver and he was issued warning.
