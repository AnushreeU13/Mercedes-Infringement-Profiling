No / Driver 44 - Lewis Hamilton Session Qualifying Fact Allegedly driving unnecessarily slowly during Qualifying. Offence Alleged breach of Article 33.4 of FIA Formula One Sporting Regulations. Decision No further action. Reason Stewards heard from driver of Car 44 (Lewis Hamilton), driver of Car 4 (Lando Norris) and team representatives, and examined video, radio communication and GPS evidence. driver, in evidence, stated that he did slow down in order to endeavour to be passed by car 4, to achieve “tow” on his fast lap, however car 4 obviously had same intent and decided not to pass him. Although car 44 was slow, Stewards note that for lap delta time was respected, driver pulled over to left and off racing line, this occurred on straight where visibility was not issue and there was no potential danger at any point, to any other driver. three cars on this portion of track at time (cars 44, 4 and 3) were all on “out” laps.
