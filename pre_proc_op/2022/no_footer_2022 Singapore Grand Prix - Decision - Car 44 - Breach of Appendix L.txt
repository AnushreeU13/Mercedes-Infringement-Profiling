No / Driver 44 - Lewis Hamilton Session Practice 3 Fact Wearing of jewellery during competition. Offence Breach of Appendix L, Chapter III of FIA Formula One Sporting Regulations. Decision No further action. Reason Stewards heard from driver of Car 44 (Lewis Hamilton), team representative and have reviewed video and audio evidence. Broadcast footage showed HAM wearing item of jewellery in form of body piercing (nose stud) during session. HAM admitted this fact but explained that he had been advised by his doctors not to remove it for time being. In response to request by Stewards, team produced reports from medical practitioner which confirmed HAM’s explanation. Stewards then consulted FIA Deputy Medical Delegate, Dr Ian Roberts, who viewed medical report and concurred with opinion therein. In light of extenuating circumstances, we have determined to take no further action.
