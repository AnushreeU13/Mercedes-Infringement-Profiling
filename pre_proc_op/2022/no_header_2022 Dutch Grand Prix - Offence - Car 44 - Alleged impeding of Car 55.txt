No / Driver 44 - Lewis Hamilton Competitor Mercedes-AMG Petronas F1 Team Time 13:18 Session Practice 1 Fact Allegedly unnecessary impeding of Car 55 by Car 44 at turns 12-13. Offence Alleged breach of Article 37.5 of FIA Formula One Sporting Regulations. Decision No further action. Reason Stewards heard from driver of Car 44 (Lewis Hamilton), driver of Car 55 (Carlos Sainz) and team representatives and examined video and radio evidence. Stewards determined that Car 55 was approaching turn 13 at higher speed than Car 44. Car 55 was finishing fast lap while Car 44 was preparing for next lap. During hearing, both drivers and team representatives recognised that it is well understood that impeding during free practice should be looked at in more lenient way by Stewards unless it is dangerous manoeuvre. Both drivers also highlighted that configuration of track is propitious for impeding and that Car 55 was not significantly impacted by action. Therefore, Stewards decided to take no further action. Competitors are reminded that they have right to appeal certain decisions of Stewards, in accordance with Article 15 of FIA International Sporting Code and Chapter 4 of FIA Judicial and Disciplinary Rules, within applicable time limits.
