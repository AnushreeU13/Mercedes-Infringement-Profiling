2023 CANADIAN GRAND PRIX 16 - 18 June 2023 StewardsFrom Stewards To Team Manager, Mercedes-AMG PETRONAS F1 TeamDocument 56 Date 18 June 2023 Time 15:20 Stewards, having received report from Race Director, have considered following matter and determine following: No / Driver 44 - Lewis Hamilton Competitor Mercedes-AMG PETRONAS F1 Team Time 14:19 Session Race Fact Alleged unsafe release. Infringment Alleged breach of Article 34.14 of FIA Formula One Sporting Regulations. Decision No further action. Reason Stewards reviewed, external video and in-car video and audio evidence. We note that whilst Car 14 had to perform minor braking in our view there was no risk of collision or need for significant evasive action, hence we determine there was no unsafe release. Competitors are reminded that they have right to appeal certain decisions of Stewards, in accordance with Article 15 of FIA International Sporting Code and Chapter 4 of FIA Judicial and Disciplinary Rules, within applicable time limits. Decisions of Stewards are taken independently of FIA and are based solely on relevant regulations, guidelines and evidence presented. Garry Connelly Mathieu Remmerie Enrique Bernoldi Marcel Demers
