2023 M ONACO GRAND PRIX 26 - 28 May 2023 StewardsFrom Stewards To Team Manager, Mercedes-AMG PETRONAS F1 TeamDocument 51 Date 28 May 2023 Time 16:39 Stewards, having received report from Race Director, have considered following matter and determine following: No / Driver 63 - George Russell Competitor Mercedes-AMG PETRONAS F1 Team Time 16:15 Session Race Fact Left and rejoined track in unsafe manner at turn 5. Infringment Breach of Appendix L, Chapter IV, Article 2 c) of International Sporting Code. Decision 5 second time penalty. 2 penalty points (total of 6 for 12 month period) Reason Stewards reviewed positioning/marshalling system data, video, timing, telemetry, team radio and in-car video evidence. Russell spun into runoff at Turn 5 then rejoined track in unsafe manner directly in front of car 11, Perez, thus causing collision. Competitors are reminded that they have right to appeal certain decisions of Stewards, in accordance with Article 15 of FIA International Sporting Code and Chapter 4 of FIA Judicial and Disciplinary Rules, within applicable time limits.
