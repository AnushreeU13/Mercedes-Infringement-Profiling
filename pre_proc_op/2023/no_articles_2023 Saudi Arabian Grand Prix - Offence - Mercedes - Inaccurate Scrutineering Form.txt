Document 16 Date 17 March 2023 Time 16:25 2023 SAUDI ARABIAN GRAND PRIX 17 - 19 March 2023 StewardsFrom Stewards To Team Manager, Mercedes-AMG PETRONAS F1 Team Stewards, having received report from Techncial Delegate (Document 11), heard from team representative, have considered following matter and determine following: No / Driver 44 - Lewis Hamilton Competitor Mercedes-AMG PETRONAS F1 Team Time 14:24 Fact Wearing of jewellery. Offence Breach of Appendix L, Chapter III, Article 5 of FIA International Sporting Code. Decision No further action. Reason Stewards having heard from team representative and receiving medical report from team doctor of Mercedes AMG-PETRONAS F1 Team, which requested exemption from compliance with Art. 5 on medical grounds. Stewards consulted FIA Medical Delegate, who viewed medical report, examined driver and concurred with medical opinion therein. In light of medical circumstances, we have determined to take no further action as there are medical concerns with removal of device. Competitors are reminded that they have right to appeal certain decisions of Stewards, in accordance with Article 15 of FIA International Sporting Code and Chapter 4 of FIA Judicial and Disciplinary Rules, within applicable time limits.
