2023 UNITED STATES GRAND PRIX 20 - 22 October 2023 From Stewards To Team Manager, Mercedes-AMG PETRONAS F1 TeamDocument 30 Date 21 October 2023 Time 14:35 Stewards, having received report from Race Director, have considered following matter and determine following: No / Driver 63 - George Russell Competitor Mercedes-AMG PETRONAS F1 Team Time 12:41 Session Sprint Shootout Fact Car 63 unnecessarily impeding Car 16 in turn 19 at 12:41. Infringement Breach of Article 37.5 of FIA Formula One Sporting Regulations. Decision Drop of 3 grid positions for next Sprint/Race in which driver participates. Reason Stewards heard from driver of Car 63 (George Russell), driver of Car 16 (Charles Leclerc), team representatives and reviewed positioning/marshalling system data, video, timing team radio and in-car video evidence. Car 63 entered last two corners preparing for his flying lap at end of SQ1 with Car 16 closing in quickly. Whilst team did inform driver about closing car few seconds in advance, there was no further information directly before Car 63 entered Turn 19 with Car 16 right behind. Car 63 did not move from racing line in or after Turn 19 and therefore unnecessarily impeded Car 16. Stewards note that irrespective of any information coming from team, it is driver’s responsibility to ensure that no other cars are unnecessarily impeded. Competitors are reminded that they have right to appeal certain decisions of Stewards, in accordance with Article 15 of FIA International Sporting Code and Chapter 4 of FIA Judicial and Disciplinary Rules, within applicable time limits. Decisions of Stewards are taken independently of FIA and are based solely on relevant regulations, guidelines and evidence presented. StewardsFelix Holter Andrew Mallalieu Derek Warwick Dennis Dean
