2023 UNITED STATES GRAND PRIX 20 - 22 October 2023 StewardsFrom Stewards To Team Manager, Mercedes-AMG PETRONAS F1 TeamDocument 39 Date 21 October 2023 Time 18:21 Stewards, having received report from Race Director, have considered following matter and determine following: No / Driver 63 - George Russell Competitor Mercedes-AMG PETRONAS F1 Team Time 17:08 Session Sprint Fact Car 63 left track at T18 and gained lasting advantage. Infringement Breach of Appendix L, Chapter IV, Article 2 c) of International Sporting Code and Article 33.3 of FIA Formula One Sporting Regulations. Decision 5 second time penalty. Reason Stewards reviewed video, timing and in-car video evidence. Car 63 was attempting to pass Car 81 on outside into Turn 15. In doing so Car 63 went off track and completed overtaking off track. At no time while still being on track was Car 63 in front of Car 81 and therefore did not have right to line, but gained lasting advantage by completing overtaking off track. Competitors are reminded that they have right to appeal certain decisions of Stewards, in accordance with Article 15 of FIA International Sporting Code and Chapter 4 of FIA Judicial and Disciplinary Rules, within applicable time limits. Decisions of Stewards are taken independently of FIA and are based solely on relevant regulations, guidelines and evidence presented. Felix Holter Andrew Mallalieu Derek Warwick Dennis Dean
