No / Driver 44 - Lewis Hamilton Fact Wearing of jewellery Offence Breach of Appendix L, Chapter III , Article 5 of FIA International Sporting Code. Decision No further action Reason Stewards having heard from team representative and receiving medical report from team doctor of Mercedes AMG-PETRONAS F1 Team, which requested exemption. Stewards consulted FIA Medical Delegate, who viewed medical report, examined driver and concurred with opinion therein. We have determined to take no further action as there are concerns about disfigurement with frequent attempts at removal of device. Competitors are reminded that they have right to appeal certain decisions of Stewards, in accordance with Article 15 of FIA International Sporting Code and Chapter 4 of FIA Judicial and Disciplinary Rules, within applicable time limits.
