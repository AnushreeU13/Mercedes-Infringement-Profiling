No / Driver 44 - Lewis Hamilton Session Sprint Fact Causing collision. Infringement Breach of Appendix L, Chapter IV, Article 2 d) of FIA International Sporting Code. Decision 5 second time penalty. (5 seconds added to elapsed Sprint time). 2 penalty points (total of 2 for 12 month period) Reason Stewards reviewed video and in-car video evidence. Hamilton was attempting to pass Perez on inside at Turn 15. While Perez was giving little room on inside for Hamilton, Hamilton drove onto kerb and subsequently understeered into Perez in wet conditions. Stewards consider that Hamilton was predominantly at fault for causing collision and order 5 second penalty. Competitors are reminded that they have right to appeal certain decisions of Stewards, in accordance with Article 15 of FIA International Sporting Code and Chapter 4 of FIA Judicial and Disciplinary Rules, within applicable time limits. Decisions of Stewards are taken independently of FIA and are based solely on relevant regulations, guidelines and evidence presented. Tim Mayer Mathieu Remmerie Derek Warwick Loïc Bacquelaine
