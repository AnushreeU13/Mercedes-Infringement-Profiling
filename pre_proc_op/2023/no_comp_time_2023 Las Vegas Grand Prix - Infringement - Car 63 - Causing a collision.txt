No / Driver 63 - George Russell Session Race Fact Car 63 collided with Car 1 at Turn 12. Infringement Breach of Appendix L, Chapter IV, Article 2 d) of FIA International Sporting Code. Decision 5 second time penalty. 2 penalty points (total of 4 for 12 month period). Reason Stewards reviewed positioning/marshalling system data, video and in-car video evidence. Car 1 was gaining on Car 63 on approach to Turn 12, and had significant portion of car alongside Car 63 at apex. driver of Car 63 appeared not to expect Car 1 to overtake in turn and subsequently, in turning into corner, collided with Car 1. Competitors are reminded that they have right to appeal certain decisions of Stewards, in accordance with Article 15 of FIA International Sporting Code and Chapter 4 of FIA Judicial and Disciplinary Rules, within applicable time limits. Decisions of Stewards are taken independently of FIA and are based solely on relevant regulations, guidelines and evidence presented. Garry Connelly Loïc Bacquelaine Derek Warwick Dennis Dean
