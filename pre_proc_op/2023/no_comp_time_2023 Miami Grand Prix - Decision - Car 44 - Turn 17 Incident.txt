No / Driver 44 - Lewis Hamilton Session Qualifying Fact Incident in turn 17. Infringment Alleged breach of Article 33.4 and 37.5 of FIA Formula One Sporting Regulations. Decision No further action. Reason Stewards heard from driver of Car 20 (Kevin Magnussen), driver of Car 44 (Lewis Hamilton), team representatives and reviewed video, team radio and in-car video evidence and determined that Car 20 was on slow lap, with another slow car in front of it. Car 44 was on in-lap and could see Car 20 at end of straight. unexpectedly high speed differential between two cars resulted in Car 44 having to take evasive action to avoid collision. Given that neither car was on fast lap and fact that Car 20 had another slow car ahead of it, we did not consider either driver to be driving dangerously. Given that they were not on fast laps, there was no question of impeding either. So in circumstances, we decided to take no further action. Competitors are reminded that they have right to appeal certain decisions of Stewards, in accordance with Article 15 of FIA International Sporting Code and Chapter 4 of FIA Judicial and Disciplinary Rules, within applicable time limits.
