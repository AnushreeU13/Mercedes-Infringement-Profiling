No / Driver 63 - George Russell Session Qualifying Fact Car 63 abnormally changed direction directly causing impact with Car 44 on start/finish straight at 16:48. Infringment Breach of Article 2b Chapter IV, Appendix L of FIA International Sporting Code. Decision Formal warning to driver and team. Reason Stewards heard from driver of Car 63 (George Russell), driver of Car 44 (Lewis Hamilton), team representatives and reviewed positioning/marshalling system data, video, team radio and in-car video evidence. driver of Car 63 stated that as he was just starting his fast lap he was looking forward and had not checked his mirrors. His team did not inform him of approach of Car 44. However in mitigation, he was reacting to car in front of him (Car 55) which had just finished its fast lap. In addition at end of his out lap driver of Car 63 had to slow significantly to avoid impeding Cars 1 and 55. Competitors are reminded that they have right to appeal certain decisions of Stewards, in accordance with Article 15 of FIA International Sporting Code and Chapter 4 of FIA Judicial and Disciplinary Rules, within applicable time limits. Decisions of Stewards are taken independently of FIA and are based solely on relevant regulations, guidelines and evidence presented. Garry Connelly Felix Holter Derek Warwick David Domingo
