2023 SPANISH GRAND PRIX 02 - 04 June 2023 StewardsFrom Stewards To Team Manager, Mercedes-AMG PETRONAS F1 TeamDocument 53 Date 04 June 2023 Time 19:05 Stewards, having received report from Media Delegate, summoned (document 51) and heard from team representative and tr , have considered following matter and determine following: Session Race Fact Driver physiotherapists/assistants in Parc Ferme Infringment Breach of Article 12.2.1 i) of International Sporting Code. Decision competitor (Mercedes-AMG PETRONAS F1 Team) is fined €10,000. Reason Stewards received report from Media Delegate, which was subsequently confirmed by video evidence, that physios / drivers’ assistants of Cars 44 and 63 entered parc ferme in violation of Post Race Interview and Podium Ceremony Procedure that was published prior to race “for orderly conduct of event.” Article 9.15.1 of FIA International Sporting Code places responsibility for actions on part of any person providing service, on Competitor to whom service is provided. Competitors are reminded that they have right to appeal certain decisions of Stewards, in accordance with Article 15 of FIA International Sporting Code and Chapter 4 of FIA Judicial and Disciplinary Rules, within applicable time limits. Decisions of Stewards are taken independently of FIA and are based solely on relevant regulations, guidelines and evidence presented. Garry Connelly Felix Holter Derek Warwick David Domingo
