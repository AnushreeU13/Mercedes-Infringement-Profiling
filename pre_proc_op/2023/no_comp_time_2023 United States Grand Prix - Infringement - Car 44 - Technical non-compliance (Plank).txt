Infringement Breach of Article 3.5.9 e) of FIA Formula One Technical Regulations. Decision Car 44 is disqualified from Race (Article 12.4.1.m of FIA International Sporting Code). other competitors move up in classification. Reason Stewards heard from team representatives of Car 44, Technical Delegate, FIA Single Seater Director and FIA Single Seater Technical Director. During hearing team acknowledged that measurement performed by FIA Technical Team was correct and stated that high wear on skid pads was probably result of unique combination of bumpy track and Sprint race schedule that minimized time to set up and check car before race. Stewards note that onus is on competitor to ensure that car is in compliance with regulations at all times during event. In this particular case, rear skid in area defined in Technical Delegate’s report was outside of thresholds outlined in Article 3.5.9 e) of FIA Formula One Technical Regulations, which includes tolerance for wear. Therefore, standard penalty for breach of Technical Regulations is imposed. Competitors are reminded that they have right to appeal certain decisions of Stewards, in accordance with Article 15 of FIA International Sporting Code and Chapter 4 of FIA Judicial and Disciplinary Rules, within applicable time limits.
