No / Driver 63 - George Russell Session Sprint Fact Car 63 left track at T18 and gained lasting advantage. Infringement Breach of Appendix L, Chapter IV, Article 2 c) of International Sporting Code and Article 33.3 of FIA Formula One Sporting Regulations. Decision 5 second time penalty. Reason Stewards reviewed video, timing and in-car video evidence. Car 63 was attempting to pass Car 81 on outside into Turn 15. In doing so Car 63 went off track and completed overtaking off track. At no time while still being on track was Car 63 in front of Car 81 and therefore did not have right to line, but gained lasting advantage by completing overtaking off track. Competitors are reminded that they have right to appeal certain decisions of Stewards, in accordance with Article 15 of FIA International Sporting Code and Chapter 4 of FIA Judicial and Disciplinary Rules, within applicable time limits. Decisions of Stewards are taken independently of FIA and are based solely on relevant regulations, guidelines and evidence presented. Felix Holter Andrew Mallalieu Derek Warwick Dennis Dean
