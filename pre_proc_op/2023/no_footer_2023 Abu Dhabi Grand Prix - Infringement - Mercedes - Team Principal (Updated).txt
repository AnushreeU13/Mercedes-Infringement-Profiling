2023 ABU DHABI GRAND PRIX 24 - 26 November 2023 Having been delegated, by Stewards of 2023 Las Vegas Grand Prix, authority in accordance with Article 11.9.3.w of FIA International Sporting Code, and having received delegated authority from USA ASN (ACCUS) to UAE ASN steward, to investigate report from FIA Media Delegate in relation to language used by Team Principal of Mercedes- AMG Petronas F1 Team during FIA Press Conference on Thursday 16 November 2023, and having summonsed Team Principal (document 6) and having received submission from FIA and examined transcript of relevant part of Press Conference, Stewards determine that; 1. breach of Article 12.2.1.f of FIA International Sporting Code has been committed by Team Principal in that language used is not consistent with values defended by FIA; 2. Based on submission from FIA, FIA regards language of this type to be unacceptable moving forward, particularly when used by participants in sport who have high public profile and who are seen by many, especially younger, followers of sport, as role models, and that in future FIA will not tolerate use of such language in FIA forums by any stakeholder; 3. Based on submission from Team Principal, use of language concerned was in this case unusual and was provoked by abrupt interjection during Press Conference and therefore cannot be regarded as typical from this Team Principal; 4. Considering mitigating circumstances in point 3 above, Stewards in this case issue formal Warning.
