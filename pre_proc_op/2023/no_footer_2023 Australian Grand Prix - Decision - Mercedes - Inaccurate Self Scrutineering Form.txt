No / Driver 44 - Lewis Hamilton Session Practice 1 Fact Wearing of jewellery Offence Breach of Appendix L, Chapter III, Article 5 of FIA International Sporting Code. Decision No further action Reason Stewards having heard from team representative and receiving medical report from team doctor of Mercedes-AMG PETRONAS F1 Team, which requested exemption from compliance with Art. 5 on medical grounds. Stewards consulted FIA Medical Delegate, who viewed medical report and concurred with medical opinion therein. In light of medical circumstances, we have determined to take no further action as there are medical concerns with removal of device.
