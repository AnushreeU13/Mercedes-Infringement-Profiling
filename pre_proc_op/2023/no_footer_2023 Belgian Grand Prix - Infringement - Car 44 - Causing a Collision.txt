No / Driver 44 - Lewis Hamilton Session Sprint Fact Causing collision. Infringement Breach of Appendix L, Chapter IV, Article 2 d) of FIA International Sporting Code. Decision 5 second time penalty. (5 seconds added to elapsed Sprint time). 2 penalty points (total of 2 for 12 month period) Reason Stewards reviewed video and in-car video evidence. Hamilton was attempting to pass Perez on inside at Turn 15. While Perez was giving little room on inside for Hamilton, Hamilton drove onto kerb and subsequently understeered into Perez in wet conditions. Stewards consider that Hamilton was predominantly at fault for causing collision and order 5 second penalty.
