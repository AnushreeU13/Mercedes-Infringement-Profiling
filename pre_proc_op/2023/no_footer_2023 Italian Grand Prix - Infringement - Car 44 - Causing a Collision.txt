No / Driver 44 - Lewis Hamilton Session Race Fact Causing a collision. Infringement Breach of Appendix L, Chapter IV, Article 2 d) of the FIA International Sporting Code. Decision 5 second time penalty. 2 penalty points (total of 4 for the 12 month period). Reason The Stewards reviewed CCTV video, video and in-car video evidence. While overtaking Piastri at the entry to turn 4, Hamilton moved to the right in the braking zone and made contact with Piastri while Piastri was moving to the right away from him and was close to the side of the track.
