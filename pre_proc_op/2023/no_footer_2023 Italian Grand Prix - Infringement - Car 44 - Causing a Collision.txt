No / Driver 44 - Lewis Hamilton Session Race Fact Causing collision. Infringement Breach of Appendix L, Chapter IV, Article 2 d) of FIA International Sporting Code. Decision 5 second time penalty. 2 penalty points (total of 4 for 12 month period). Reason Stewards reviewed CCTV video, video and in-car video evidence. While overtaking Piastri at entry to turn 4, Hamilton moved to right in braking zone and made contact with Piastri while Piastri was moving to right away from him and was close to side of track.
