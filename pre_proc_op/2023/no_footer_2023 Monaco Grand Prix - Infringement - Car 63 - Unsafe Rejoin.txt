No / Driver 63 - George Russell Session Race Fact Left and rejoined track in unsafe manner at turn 5. Infringment Breach of Appendix L, Chapter IV, Article 2 c) of International Sporting Code. Decision 5 second time penalty. 2 penalty points (total of 6 for 12 month period) Reason Stewards reviewed positioning/marshalling system data, video, timing, telemetry, team radio and in-car video evidence. Russell spun into runoff at Turn 5 then rejoined track in unsafe manner directly in front of car 11, Perez, thus causing collision.
