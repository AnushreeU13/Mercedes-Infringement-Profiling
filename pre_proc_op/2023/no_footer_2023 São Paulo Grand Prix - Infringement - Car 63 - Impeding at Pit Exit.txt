No / Driver 63 - George Russell Session Qualifying Fact Car 63 was driving slowly in pit exit and was not as far to left as possible. Infringement Breaches of Article 33.4 of FIA Formula One Sporting Regulations and Article 12.2.1 i) of International Sporting Code and non-compliance with Race Director’s Event Note (item 14, document 4). Decision Drop of 2 grid positions for next Race in which driver participates. Reason Stewards heard from driver of Car 63 (George Russell), team representative and reviewed in-car video evidence. In order to avoid situations like ones that occurred in Mexico, Race Director’s Event Notes for this event contained specific clause (item 14) stipulating that it was permitted to go slow in pit exit to create gap before crossing SC2 line, however, by doing so, driver must stay as far to left as possible to allow other drivers to pass on right side. RUS was exiting pits, preparing for out lap. RUS went slow to create gap for clear lap, but did not manage to stay completely to left. As result, following car(s) were not able to overtake, as intended by Race Director’s instructions. This clearly violates wording and spirit of item 14 of Race Director’s Event Notes.
