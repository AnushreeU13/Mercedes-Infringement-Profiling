No / Driver 63 - George Russell Session Sprint Shootout Fact Car 63 unnecessarily impeding Car 16 in turn 19 at 12:41. Infringement Breach of Article 37.5 of FIA Formula One Sporting Regulations. Decision Drop of 3 grid positions for next Sprint/Race in which driver participates. Reason Stewards heard from driver of Car 63 (George Russell), driver of Car 16 (Charles Leclerc), team representatives and reviewed positioning/marshalling system data, video, timing team radio and in-car video evidence. Car 63 entered last two corners preparing for his flying lap at end of SQ1 with Car 16 closing in quickly. Whilst team did inform driver about closing car few seconds in advance, there was no further information directly before Car 63 entered Turn 19 with Car 16 right behind. Car 63 did not move from racing line in or after Turn 19 and therefore unnecessarily impeded Car 16. Stewards note that irrespective of any information coming from team, it is driver’s responsibility to ensure that no other cars are unnecessarily impeded.
