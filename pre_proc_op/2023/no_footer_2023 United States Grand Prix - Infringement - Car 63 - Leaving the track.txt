No / Driver 63 - George Russell Session Sprint Fact Car 63 left track at T18 and gained lasting advantage. Infringement Breach of Appendix L, Chapter IV, Article 2 c) of International Sporting Code and Article 33.3 of FIA Formula One Sporting Regulations. Decision 5 second time penalty. Reason Stewards reviewed video, timing and in-car video evidence. Car 63 was attempting to pass Car 81 on outside into Turn 15. In doing so Car 63 went off track and completed overtaking off track. At no time while still being on track was Car 63 in front of Car 81 and therefore did not have right to line, but gained lasting advantage by completing overtaking off track.
