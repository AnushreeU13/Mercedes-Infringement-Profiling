No / Driver 44 - Lewis Hamilton Competitor Mercedes-AMG PETRONAS F1 Team Time 16:22 Session Race Fact Causing collision. Infringement Breach of Appendix L, Chapter IV, Article 2 d) of FIA International Sporting Code. Decision 5 second time penalty. 2 penalty points (total of 4 for 12 month period). Reason Stewards reviewed CCTV video, video and in-car video evidence. While overtaking Piastri at entry to turn 4, Hamilton moved to right in braking zone and made contact with Piastri while Piastri was moving to right away from him and was close to side of track. Competitors are reminded that they have right to appeal certain decisions of Stewards, in accordance with Article 15 of FIA International Sporting Code and Chapter 4 of FIA Judicial and Disciplinary Rules, within applicable time limits. Decisions of Stewards are taken independently of FIA and are based solely on relevant regulations, guidelines and evidence presented. Tim Mayer Loïc Bacquelaine Vitantonio Liuzzi Matteo Perini
