No / Driver 63 - George Russell Competitor Mercedes-AMG PETRONAS F1 Team Time 15:51 Session Race Fact Car 63 left track at T1 and gained lasting advantage. Infringement Breach of Appendix L, Chapter IV, Article 2 c) of International Sporting Code. Decision 5 second time penalty. Reason Stewards reviewed video, CCTV video, timing and in-car video evidence. Russell left pits, and was on inside of Ocon at entry to turn one. However, as Russel approached apex, he showed some lockup and went straight entering runoff at turn one and rejoined after turn 2, passing Ocon while doing so and gaining both position and time. Russell did not give back position and thus gained lasting advantage. Competitors are reminded that they have right to appeal certain decisions of Stewards, in accordance with Article 15 of FIA International Sporting Code and Chapter 4 of FIA Judicial and Disciplinary Rules, within applicable time limits. Decisions of Stewards are taken independently of FIA and are based solely on relevant regulations, guidelines and evidence presented. Tim Mayer Loïc Bacquelaine Vitantonio Liuzzi Matteo Perini
