No / Driver 44 - Lewis Hamilton Competitor Mercedes-AMG PETRONAS F1 Team Time 20:06 Session Race Fact driver of Car 44 crossed live track to return to pit lane. Infringement Breach of Article 26.7 b) of FIA Formula One Sporting Regulations. Decision Reprimand (Non-driving) This is driver’s 1st reprimand of season. driver is fined €50,000, €25,000 of which is suspended for remainder of 2023 season on condition that there is no further breach of similar nature. Reason Stewards heard from driver of Car ​​44 ( Lewis Hamilton), team representative and reviewed video evidence. After crashing out of race in Lap 1, driver of Car 44 abandoned his car in gravel and ran back to pits. He thereby crossed track that was live at this time and reached inside edge of track just seconds before Car 63 arrived at high speed after exiting pits. He then continued to walk alongside track until finally exiting track. During hearing driver of Car 44 was very apologetic and realized that situation could have been very dangerous for him as well as drivers approaching. Stewards reinforced fact that crossing live track can cause extremely dangerous situations and drivers have to be very cautious about it. In addition to imposing significant fine (which is suspended in parts), Stewards also reprimand driver bearing in mind that receiving certain number of reprimands during season will draw significant driving penalty in accordance with Article 18.2 of FIA Formula One Sporting Regulations. Competitors are reminded that they have right to appeal certain decisions of Stewards, in accordance with Article 15 of FIA International Sporting Code and Chapter 4 of FIA Judicial and Disciplinary Rules, within applicable time limits.
