2024 AUSTRIAN GRAND PRIX 28 - 30 June 2024 StewardsFrom Stewards To Team Manager, Mercedes-AMG PETRONAS F1 TeamDocument 49 Date 29 June 2024 Time 19:44 Stewards, having received report from Race Director, summoned (document 40) and heard from driver and team representative, have considered following matter and determine following: No / Driver 44 - Lewis Hamilton Competitor Mercedes-AMG PETRONAS F1 Team Time 16:50 Session Qualifying Fact Unsafe release of Car 44. Infringement Breach of Article 34.14 of FIA Formula One Sporting Regulations. Decision competitor (Mercedes-AMG PETRONAS F1 Team) is fined €5,000. Reason Stewards heard from team representative and reviewed video evidence. Car 44 was released from garage in unsafe manner dragging jack and exhaust extractor behind it. Stewards acknowledge that team immediately informed driver to stop to prevent any further damage and/or dangerous situation. Competitors are reminded that they have right to appeal certain decisions of Stewards, in accordance with Article 15 of FIA International Sporting Code and Chapter 4 of FIA Judicial and Disciplinary Rules, within applicable time limits. Decisions of Stewards are taken independently of FIA and are based solely on relevant regulations, guidelines and evidence presented. Felix Holter Matthew Selley Johnny Herbert Wilhelm Singer
