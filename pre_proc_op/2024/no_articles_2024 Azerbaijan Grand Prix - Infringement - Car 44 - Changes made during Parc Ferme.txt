2024 AZERBAIJAN GRAND PRIX 13 - 15 September 2024 StewardsFrom Stewards To Team Manager, Mercedes-AMG PETRONAS F1 TeamDocument 49 Date 15 September 2024 Time 12:49 Stewards, having received report from Technical Delegate (document 46), have considered following matter and determine following: No / Driver 44 - Lewis Hamilton Competitor Mercedes-AMG PETRONAS F1 Team Time 11:17 Session Qualifying Fact following Power Unit elements have been used: 5th Internal Combustion Engine (ICE) 5th Turbo Charger (TC) 5th Motor Generator Unit - Heat (MGU-H) 5th Motor Generator Unit - Kinetic (MGU-K) All Power Unit elements were replaced without approval of FIA Technical Delegate during Parc Ferme. Infringement Breaches of Articles 28.2 and 40.3 of FIA Formula One Sporting Regulations. Decision Required to start Race from pit lane. Reason Power Unit elements used are in excess of permitted number for 2024 Championship season. This is breach of Article 28.2 of FIA Formula One Sporting Regulations. Further, parts have been replaced without approval of Technical Delegate, this being breach of Article 40.3. Therefore Car 44 is required to start Race from pit lane in accordance with Article 40.9. Competitors are reminded that they have right to appeal certain decisions of Stewards, in accordance with Article 15 of FIA International Sporting Code and Chapter 4 of FIA Judicial and Disciplinary Rules, within applicable time limits. Decisions of Stewards are taken independently of FIA and are based solely on relevant regulations, guidelines and evidence presented. Tim Mayer Matteo Perini Johnny Herbert Danil Solomin
