2024 BELGIAN GRAND PRIX 26 - 28 July 2024 From Stewards To Team Manager, Mercedes-AMG PETRONAS F1 TeamDocument 44 Date 28 July 2024 Time 18:48 Stewards, having received report from Technical Delegate (document 42), summoned (document 43) and heard from team representative, FIA Technical Delegate, FIA Single Seater Director and FIA Single Seater Technical Director, have considered following matter and determine following: No / Driver 63 - George Russell Competitor Mercedes-AMG PETRONAS F1 Team Time 17:50 Session Race Fact Weight of Car 63 (796.5 kg) was below minimum weight required (798.0 kg). Infringement Breach of Article 4.1 of FIA Formula One Technical Regulations. Decision Car 63 is disqualified from Race classification. All other drivers move up in classification. Reason Stewards heard from team representative of Car 63 (George Russell), FIA Technical Delegate, FIA Single Seater Director and FIA Single Seater Technical Director. Car 63 was weighed on FIA inside and outside scales with both scales showing same result of 796.5 kg. calibration of both scales was confirmed and witnessed by competitor. During hearing team representative confirmed that measurement is correct and that all required procedures were performed correctly. team also acknowledged that there were no mitigating circumstances and that it was genuine error by team. Stewards determine that Article 4.1 of FIA Formula 1 Technical Regulations has been breached and therefore standard penalty for such infringement needs to be applied. Competitors are reminded that they have right to appeal certain decisions of Stewards, in accordance with Article 15 of FIA International Sporting Code and Chapter 4 of FIA Judicial and Disciplinary Rules, within applicable time limits. Decisions of Stewards are taken independently of FIA and are based solely on relevant regulations, guidelines and evidence presented.
