2024 M IAMI GRAND PRIX 03 - 05 May 2024 From Stewards To Team Manager, Mercedes-AMG PETRONAS F1 TeamDocument 41 Date 04 May 2024 Time 13:24 Stewards, having received report from Race Director, have considered following matter and determine following: No / Driver 44 - Lewis Hamilton Competitor Mercedes-AMG PETRONAS F1 Team Time 13:12 Session Sprint Fact Pit lane speeding – 90.7 km/h Infringement Breach of Article 34.7 of FIA Formula One Sporting Regulations. Decision Drive through penalty. (20 seconds added to elapsed Sprint time). Reason Car 44 exceeded pit lane speed limit which is set at 80 km/h for this event by 10.7 km/h. Per Penalty Guidelines, penalty for speeding in pit lane during sprint session race by between 6-15km/h is drive through. This is what we impose. As drive through penalty was imposed during last three laps, it will be converted to 20 second time penalty per Article 54.3 (d) of Formula One Sporting Regulations. Competitors are reminded that they have right to appeal certain decisions of Stewards, in accordance with Article 15 of FIA International Sporting Code and Chapter 4 of FIA Judicial and Disciplinary Rules, within applicable time limits. Decisions of Stewards are taken independently of FIA and are based solely on relevant regulations, guidelines and evidence presented.
