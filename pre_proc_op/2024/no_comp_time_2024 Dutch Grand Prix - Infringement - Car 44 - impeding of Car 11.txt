No / Driver 44 - Lewis Hamilton Session Qualifying Fact Car 44 unnecessarily impeded Car 11 in turn 9. Infringement Breach of Article 37.5 of FIA Formula One Sporting Regulations. Decision Drop of 3 grid positions for next Race in which driver participates. Reason Stewards heard from driver of Car 11 (Sergio Perez), driver of Car 44 (Lewis Hamilton), team representatives and reviewed positioning/marshalling system data, video, timing, telemetry, team radio and in-car video evidence. driver of Car 44, being on in lap, was informed by team that Car 11 was approaching on fast lap when he entered Turn 8. He then drove off line at exit of Turn 8 with intention to give way for Car 11. However, when Car 11 arrived, Car 44 had already entered Turn 9 and drove back towards racing line at exit of Turn 9, thereby clearly impeding Car 11. Stewards determine that, whilst there has been appropriate warning by team and albeit driver tried to move out of way, he could have slowed down more in order not to impede other car and therefore consider impeding to be unnecessary in sense of regulations. Therefore grid drop is applied in line with previous decisions. Competitors are reminded that they have right to appeal certain decisions of Stewards, in accordance with Article 15 of FIA International Sporting Code and Chapter 4 of FIA Judicial and Disciplinary Rules, within applicable time limits. Decisions of Stewards are taken independently of FIA and are based solely on relevant regulations, guidelines and evidence presented. StewardsFelix Holter Matteo Perini Johnny Herbert Arie Kroeze
