No / Driver 44 - Lewis Hamilton Session Practice 2 Fact Car 44 unnecessarily impeding Car 2 in Turn 11 at 20:16. Infringement Breach of Article 37.5 of FIA Formula One Sporting Regulations. Decision Warning. competitor (Mercedes-AMG PETRONAS F1 Team) is fined €15,000. Reason Stewards heard from driver of Car 44 (Lewis Hamilton), driver of Car 2 (Logan Sargeant), team representatives and reviewed positioning/marshalling system data, video, team radio and in-car video evidence and determine that Car 44 impeded Car 2 at Turn 11. As result, Car 2 had to take evasive action by going off track to avoid collision. Had that not been done, there would have been serious, high speed crash. Having listened to team radio, it was clear to us that team of Car 44 failed to warn their driver of fact that Car 2 was arriving on fast lap. That was serious failure on part of team, particularly given speeds on this circuit and nature of turn 11, which is at end of series of high speed corners where driver visibility is impaired. We therefore issue warning to driver and impose fine of €15,000 to team. Competitors are reminded that they have right to appeal certain decisions of Stewards, in accordance with Article 15 of FIA International Sporting Code and Chapter 4 of FIA Judicial and Disciplinary Rules, within applicable time limits. Decisions of Stewards are taken independently of FIA and are based solely on relevant regulations, guidelines and evidence presented.
