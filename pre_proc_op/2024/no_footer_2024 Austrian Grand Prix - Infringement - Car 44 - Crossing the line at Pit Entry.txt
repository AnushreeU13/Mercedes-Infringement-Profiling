No / Driver 44 - Lewis Hamilton Session Race Fact Car 44 crossed the white line at pit entry. Infringement Breach of Appendix L, Chapter IV Article 4 d) of the International Sporting Code and non-compliance with Race Director’s Event Notes (item 12.2, document 16). Decision 5 second time penalty. Reason The Stewards reviewed video evidence. Car 44 crossed the white line at pit entry when entering the pits. The manoeuvre was not considered dangerous in any way, therefore the least severe penalty available is imposed.
