No / Driver 44 - Lewis Hamilton Session Qualifying Fact Unsafe release of Car 44. Infringement Breach of Article 34.14 of FIA Formula One Sporting Regulations. Decision competitor (Mercedes-AMG PETRONAS F1 Team) is fined €5,000. Reason Stewards heard from team representative and reviewed video evidence. Car 44 was released from garage in unsafe manner dragging jack and exhaust extractor behind it. Stewards acknowledge that team immediately informed driver to stop to prevent any further damage and/or dangerous situation.
