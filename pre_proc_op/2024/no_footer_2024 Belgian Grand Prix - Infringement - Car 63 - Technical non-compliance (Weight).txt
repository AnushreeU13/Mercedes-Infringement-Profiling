No / Driver 63 - George Russell Session Race Fact Weight of Car 63 (796.5 kg) was below minimum weight required (798.0 kg). Infringement Breach of Article 4.1 of FIA Formula One Technical Regulations. Decision Car 63 is disqualified from Race classification. All other drivers move up in classification. Reason Stewards heard from team representative of Car 63 (George Russell), FIA Technical Delegate, FIA Single Seater Director and FIA Single Seater Technical Director. Car 63 was weighed on FIA inside and outside scales with both scales showing same result of 796.5 kg. calibration of both scales was confirmed and witnessed by competitor. During hearing team representative confirmed that measurement is correct and that all required procedures were performed correctly. team also acknowledged that there were no mitigating circumstances and that it was genuine error by team. Stewards determine that Article 4.1 of FIA Formula 1 Technical Regulations has been breached and therefore standard penalty for such infringement needs to be applied.
