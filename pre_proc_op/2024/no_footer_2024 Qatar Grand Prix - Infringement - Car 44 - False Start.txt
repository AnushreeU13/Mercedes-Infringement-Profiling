No / Driver 44 - Lewis Hamilton Session Race Fact False start. Infringement Breach of Article 48.1 of FIA Formula One Sporting Regulations. Decision 5 second time penalty. Reason Stewards reviewed positioning/marshalling system data, video, timing and in-car video evidence. FIA transponder indicated false start. This was confirmed by examination of video evidence from on-board camera of Car 44. As start of Car 44 was only very slightly ahead of start signal, minimum penalty is applied.
