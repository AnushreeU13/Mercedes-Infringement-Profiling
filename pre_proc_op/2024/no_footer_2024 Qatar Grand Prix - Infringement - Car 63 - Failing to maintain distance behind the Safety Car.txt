No / Driver 63 - George Russell Session Race Fact driver of Car 63 failed to maintain 10 car-length distance behind Safety Car. Infringement Breach of Article 55.7 of FIA Formula One Sporting Regulations. Decision 5 second time penalty. 1 penalty point (total of 1 penalty point for 12 month period) Reason Stewards reviewed positioning/marshalling system data, video, timing and in-car video evidence. evidence clearly showed that Car 63 was more than 125 metres, which is more than 20 car lengths, behind car in front during Safety Car period.
