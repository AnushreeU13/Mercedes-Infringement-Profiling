No / Driver 63 - George Russell Session Race Fact Forcing car 77 off track at turn 12. Infringement Breach of Appendix L, Chapter IV, Article 2 b) of International Sporting Code. Decision 5 second time penalty. Reason Stewards reviewed positioning/marshalling system data and in-car video evidence. Driving Standard Guidelines provide that when overtaking on inside driver must not force other car off track and must leave fair and acceptable width for car being overtaken. This did not occur on this occasion. However in mitigation, 5 second penalty is imposed instead of standard 10 second penalty because Stewards determine that forcing off track was not deliberate, and driver of Car 63 was in control of car at all times.
