No / Driver 63 - George Russell Competitor Mercedes-AMG PETRONAS F1 Team Time 15:05 Session Qualifying Fact Unsafe release of Car 63. Infringement Breach of Article 34.14 of FIA Formula One Sporting Regulations. Decision competitor (Mercedes-AMG PETRONAS F1 Team) is fined €5,000. Reason Stewards heard from driver of Car 63 (George Russell), driver of Car 81 (Oscar Piastri), team representatives and reviewed external and in-car video evidence. driver of Car 63 stated that on receiving release signal from his mechanic, he had intended to drive down working lane, however he saw mechanic from Car 44 in his peripheral vision and in order to avoid him, he claimed he had to move into fast lane. He was not able to see approach of Car 81. driver of Car 81 stated that he had to take avoiding action by steering away from approaching Car 63. Examination of pit lane video shows that mechanic of Car 63 who released car did not look down to pit lane to see if it was clear. Even though stated intention of team was for Car 63 to move down working lane then blend in prior to pit exit, it is still responsibility of team to release car in safe manner and good practice would dictate that prior to car being released, pit lanes (both fast and working) should be checked for approaching vehicles. Competitors are reminded that they have right to appeal certain decisions of Stewards, in accordance with Article 15 of FIA International Sporting Code and Chapter 4 of FIA Judicial and Disciplinary Rules, within applicable time limits. Decisions of Stewards are taken independently of FIA and are based solely on relevant regulations, guidelines and evidence presented.
