No / Driver 44 - Lewis Hamilton Competitor Mercedes-AMG PETRONAS F1 Team Time 19:03 Session Race Fact False start. Infringement Breach of Article 48.1 of FIA Formula One Sporting Regulations. Decision 5 second time penalty. Reason Stewards reviewed positioning/marshalling system data, video, timing and in-car video evidence. FIA transponder indicated false start. This was confirmed by examination of video evidence from on-board camera of Car 44. As start of Car 44 was only very slightly ahead of start signal, minimum penalty is applied. Competitors are reminded that they have right to appeal certain decisions of Stewards, in accordance with Article 15 of FIA International Sporting Code and Chapter 4 of FIA Judicial and Disciplinary Rules, within applicable time limits.
