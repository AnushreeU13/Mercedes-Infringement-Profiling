No / Driver 63 - George Russell Competitor Mercedes-AMG PETRONAS F1 Team Time 20:10 Session Race Fact driver of Car 63 failed to maintain 10 car-length distance behind Safety Car. Infringement Breach of Article 55.7 of FIA Formula One Sporting Regulations. Decision 5 second time penalty. 1 penalty point (total of 1 penalty point for 12 month period) Reason Stewards reviewed positioning/marshalling system data, video, timing and in-car video evidence. evidence clearly showed that Car 63 was more than 125 metres, which is more than 20 car lengths, behind car in front during Safety Car period. Competitors are reminded that they have right to appeal certain decisions of Stewards, in accordance with Article 15 of FIA International Sporting Code and Chapter 4 of FIA Judicial and Disciplinary Rules, within applicable time limits. Decisions of Stewards are taken independently of FIA and are based solely on relevant regulations, guidelines and evidence presented. Garry Connelly Mathieu Remmerie Derek Warwick Amro Al Hamad
