No / Driver 63 - George Russell Competitor Mercedes-AMG PETRONAS F1 Team Time 13:13 Session Race Fact driver left grid although aborted start signal was given. Infringement Breach of Article 47.1 of FIA Formula One Sporting Regulations. Decision Reprimand (Driving). driver is fined €5,000. Reason Stewards heard from team representative and reviewed video evidence. FIA Formula One Race Director initiated Aborted Start procedure indicating that drivers should not leave grid. This message was necessary as there was car off track in Turn 4 (STR) that needed to be recovered. Although signal was appropriately given light panel illuminated as prescribed, and teams notified by messaging system, driver left grid and proceeded on lap that he assumed to be extra formation lap. As driver was on front row of grid this triggered following drivers to take similar action. At some point Race Director realizing that for practical reasons all cars would now need to do extra formation lap gave instruction to teams for all cars to proceed and return to grid to follow correct aborted start procedure. In opinion of stewards driver RUS precipitated action of drivers on grid directly behind him. Competitors are reminded that they have right to appeal certain decisions of Stewards, in accordance with Article 15 of FIA International Sporting Code and Chapter 4 of FIA Judicial and Disciplinary Rules, within applicable time limits. Decisions of Stewards are taken independently of FIA and are based solely on relevant regulations, guidelines and evidence presented. StewardsGerd Ennser Andrew Mallalieu Johnny Herbert Luciano Burti
